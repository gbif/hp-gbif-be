The Belgian GBIF Node invites Master’s and PhD students to submit their application for the 2026 GBIF Graduate Researchers Award, which highlights outstanding research making use of GBIF-mediated biodiversity data.
Up to 2 applicants will be nominated by the Belgian node and a jury led by the GBIF Science Committee will review the nominations to select two winners.

**Deadline: 15 May 2026**

**Submission email: contact@biodiversity.be**

**How to apply**

Applicants are invited to submit the following documents:
*  Curriculum vitae
*  Summary of their research project using GBIF data
*  Short description of how GBIF-mediated data support their work
*  Any additional material they consider relevant

See the detailed [call for nomination on GBIF.org](https://www.gbif.org/news/2qFpkI7rwBNbMuSjn2rtae/2026-gbif-graduate-researchers-award-call-for-nominations)

Please send your complete application package to contact@biodiversity.be before the deadline.
